Job description

Are you passionate about advancing sustainable transportation and reducing range anxiety for EV drivers? Join Supabase, a pioneering company on a mission to revolutionize the EV charging landscape.

As a leader in Reliability-as-a-Service (RaaS), Supabase is dedicated to optimizing uptime for EV charging infrastructure by implementing cutting-edge monitoring solutions and proactive problem-solving.

About the role

As a UI/UX designer reporting directly to the CTO, your insights will have a direct impact on our product's direction. This role requires an insightful UI/UX Designer with a passion for creating seamless and engaging digital experiences. You will play a pivotal role in shaping the user experience, serving as the driving force behind the design.

What you will do
  • Design UI/UX for new features and updates, turning complex processes into smooth, user-friendly experiences.
  • Engage in everything from wireframing and storyboarding to UI testing and user feedback sessions, all while keeping the user's needs at the heart of your designs.
  • Constantly iterate on designs based on user feedback, ensuring the user experience is always improving.
  • Collaborate closely with our product team and engineers, ideating designs that impress and operate seamlessly.
What you bring to the table
  • A portfolio that showcases your brilliance in UI/UX design.
  • Experience designing data-driven apps.
  • Proficiency in Figma; familiarity with Jira, and Tailwind UI is a plus.
  • Experience with user monitoring tools such as Sentry, Pendo, or other platforms is a plus.
  • A deep understanding of user-centered design principles.
  • A dynamic, intelligent approach to problem-solving.
  • Exceptional collaboration and communication skills, paired with a burning passion for startups and entrepreneurship.
Desired Attributes
  • Passionate about sustainability and technological innovations in the EV industry.
  • Familiarity with Inclusive Design principles.
  • Graphic design skills.
What we offer
  • Competitive salary
  • Remote work
  • Generous vacation and time off package
  • A break between Christmas and New Years
  • A budget to set up your home office
  • Health insurance
  • Virtual medicine and EAP via Dialogue
  • Opportunities for professional growth and development
  • A dynamic and inclusive work environment with a focus on innovation

If you are a motivated individual looking to make a meaningful impact in the EV charging industry, join Supabase and be part of the team shaping the future of clean and reliable transportation. Apply now to be a key player in driving the success of our mission!
